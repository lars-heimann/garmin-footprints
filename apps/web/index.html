Invite-only Garmin map builder

Publish a shareable running footprint map from your Garmin export.

Raw ZIP uploads are used only for processing and are deleted after success or failure. Starts and finishes are trimmed before the public map is generated.

Garmin export
  1. Log into Garmin Connect.
  2. Open Garmin's account data export page.
  3. Request your account export.
  4. Wait for Garmin to prepare it. Garmin may take up to 30 days. In practice, many exports arrive much sooner, sometimes within about 20 minutes.
  5. Download the ZIP Garmin provides.
  6. Upload the ZIP unchanged.

Before you choose a file

The Garmin ZIP contains private health and location data. This app extracts only running GPS tracks, trims starts and finishes, deletes the raw ZIP after processing succeeds or fails, and publishes only the generated map assets at your chosen slug URL.

What file should I upload?
  • Upload the full Garmin account export ZIP.
  • Do not unzip it.
  • Do not upload a single .fit, .gpx, .tcx, or activity export.
  • Do not upload a Strava export.
  • If Garmin gives multiple files, upload the main account export archive containing DI_CONNECT.
