Invite-only Garmin map builder

Create a private preview first. Publish only if you like it.

Your Garmin ZIP never leaves your browser. You do not need an invite code to preview your map. If you choose to share it, publishing uploads only generated map files and the public map expires after 30 days.

Step 1: Private preview

Start here with your Garmin account export ZIP. Everything in this step happens locally in your browser.

  1. Log into Garmin Connect.
  2. Open Garmin's account data export page.
  3. Request your account export.
  4. Garmin emails you when the download is ready. It often takes about 15-20 minutes, but can take up to 30 days.
  5. Download the ZIP Garmin provides and upload it unchanged.

The ZIP usually has a generated name like a22756ef-76bc-4c8e-9407-5dea56a67a6b_1.zip.

Before you choose a file

The Garmin ZIP contains private health and location data. This app extracts running GPS tracks in your browser, trims starts and finishes, and previews the result locally. Publishing uploads only meta.json and points.bin.

Get your Garmin account export

Start at Garmin's official export page. You may need to log in to Garmin Connect first. Garmin says exports may take up to 30 days; in practice many arrive much sooner, often around 15-20 minutes, and Garmin emails you when the download is ready.

Open Garmin export page

  1. Log into Garmin Connect.
  2. Request your account export from Garmin's data management page.
  3. Wait for Garmin's email and download the ZIP it provides.
  4. Upload the full account export ZIP unchanged.
  5. Do not upload a single .fit, .gpx, .tcx, Strava export, or a folder you zipped yourself.
